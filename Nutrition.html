Recommended Healthy Eating Habits

Here are some tips to maintain a healthy diet:

  • Eat a variety of colorful vegetables for a range of nutrients.

  • Incorporate fresh fruits into your daily meals for natural sweetness.

  • Choose whole grains like brown rice and quinoa for added fiber.

  • Include lean proteins such as fish, poultry, beans, and nuts.

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
