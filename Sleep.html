Tips for Better Sleep

Here are some tips to improve your sleep quality:

  • Create a calming sleep environment to help signal your body that it's time to wind down.

  • Set a regular sleep schedule to regulate your body's internal clock and improve overall sleep quality.

  • Avoid screens at least an hour before bedtime to reduce exposure to blue light, which can interfere with sleep.

  • Try relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or meditation to calm your mind before sleep.

  • Avoid consuming caffeine and heavy meals close to bedtime to prevent sleep disturbances.
